Serves 10

  • 2 tsp Sweet paprika

  • 2 tsp Garlic powder

  • 2 tsp Onion powder

  • 2 tsp Dried oregano

  • 2 tsp Dried thyme

  • 2 tsp Salt

  • 1 tsp White ground pepper

  • 1 tsp Ground nutmeg

  • 1 1/4 lbs Green apples, peeled, cored & sliced thin on a mandoline

  • 1 lb Sweet potatoes, peeled & sliced thin on a mandoline

  • 2 cups Gruyere cheese, grated

  • 2/3 cups Heavy cream

Preheat convection oven to 400°F

Mix spices together.

Generously spray a deep baking dish with oil.

Arrange half of the apples and potatoes on bottom of dish. Season with half of the spice mixture. Sprinkle with half of the cheese. Repeat with remaining half of ingredients.

Pour cream over mixture. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake until cheese is golden brown – about 20 minutes longer.

Remove and let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
