Champagne Pick-Me-Up

  • 1¾ ounces brandy
  • ¾ ounce orange juice
  • ¾ ounce lemon juice
  • dash grenadine
  • chilled champagne
  1. Put ice into a cocktail shaker.
  2. Pour the brandy, orange juice, lemon juice, and grenadine over the ice and shake vigorously until well frosted.
  3. Strain into a chilled wine glass and top with champagne.
